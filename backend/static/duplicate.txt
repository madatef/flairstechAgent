Understanding Duplicate Charges

If you were charged twice for the same service, we’ll fix it for you as soon as possible.

We typically:
- Verify the payment records.
- Issue a refund for the duplicate charge.
- Send a confirmation email once the refund is processed.

Refunds can take 3–5 business days depending on your bank. If you have additional receipts or transaction IDs, replying to your ticket with them helps speed up the process.
