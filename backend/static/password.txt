How to Reset Your Password

If you're unable to log in because you forgot your password, don’t worry — resetting it is easy:

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click on "Forgot Password".
3. Enter the email associated with your account.
4. Check your inbox for a password reset link.
5. Click the link and choose a new password.

If you don’t see the email, please check your spam/junk folder. If the issue persists, feel free to reply to your ticket and we’ll assist further.
